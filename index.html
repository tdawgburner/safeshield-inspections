Why Professional Testing Matters

Legal Requirements

Virginia regulations require professional asbestos testing before renovation or demolition projects. Violations can result in fines ranging from $25,000 to $75,000 per day.

  • Potential criminal charges for property owners
  • Project delays and costly remediation
  • Insurance claim denials

Health Risks

Asbestos exposure causes devastating diseases that can remain dormant for 20-50 years:

  • Mesothelioma: Aggressive cancer with no cure
  • Lung Cancer: Significantly increased risk
  • Asbestosis: Chronic scarring of lung tissue

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does testing cost?

Professional testing typically costs a few hundred dollars depending on property size and number of samples needed. This is a fraction of the cost compared to potential fines and health risks.

How long does an inspection take?

Most residential inspections are completed within 1-2 hours. Full analysis reports are typically delivered within 3-5 business days.

When was asbestos banned?

Asbestos has never been fully banned in the United States. While most uses were phased out by the late 1980s, certain products can still legally contain asbestos today.
